# AI photo prompts — pilot button heroes

The site ships with consistent **SVG button renders** (see `ButtonSwatch`), so it
looks complete today. When you want photoreal hero images, run the prompts below in
Midjourney / DALL·E / Firefly, then drop the results into
`public/images/products/<variantSku>.jpg` and wire them in (see README → "Adding
photoreal images").

**Keep all four consistent** — same background, lighting, framing, and crop — so the
catalog reads as one cohesive set. Use the shared template and only swap the bracketed
attributes.

## Shared template
> Studio product photograph of a single [SIZE-ligne] [HOLE-TYPE] [MATERIAL] garment
> button, [FINISH] finish, centered on a seamless light-grey (#ededed) background, soft
> diffused top-left lighting, subtle contact shadow, 1:1 macro, sharp focus, shallow
> depth of field, premium e-commerce catalog style, no text, no hands, no thread.

## Per-product

**Corozo 4-Hole Classic (COR-4H-24-TORT)**
> …single 24-ligne 4-hole corozo (tagua nut) garment button, matte tortoiseshell finish,
> visible natural grain, warm brown tones…

**Urea Resin Shank — Coat (URE-SH-32-BLK)**
> …single 32-ligne domed shank urea-resin coat button, polished glossy black finish,
> metal shank loop visible from a slight three-quarter angle…

**Mother-of-Pearl 2-Hole — Shirt (SHL-2H-18-WHT)**
> …single 18-ligne 2-hole genuine trocas shell (mother-of-pearl) shirt button, natural
> white with soft iridescent lustre, subtle rainbow sheen…

**Antique Brass Shank — Jacket (MTL-SH-24-ABRS)**
> …single 24-ligne die-struck metal shank jacket button, hand-aged antique brass finish,
> crisp rim, slight three-quarter angle showing the shank…

## Notes
- Generate 3–4 candidates per product; pick the one whose lighting/scale best matches
  the others.
- Disclose mockups vs. photos honestly; the footer already carries a
  "representative visuals, samples on request" line. B2B buyers expect sample cards.
